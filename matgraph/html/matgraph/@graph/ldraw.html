PURPOSE

ldraw(g,line_style) --- draw a graph with vertices marked with their labels

SYNOPSIS

DESCRIPTION 

 ldraw(g,line_style) --- draw a graph with vertices marked with their labels
 If the graph is unlabled, we use the vertex numbers instead.
 See also draw, cdraw, and ndraw.
