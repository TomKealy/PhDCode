intersect

PURPOSE

intersect(g,h1,h2) --- g is set to the intersection of h1 and h2.

SYNOPSIS

DESCRIPTION 

 intersect(g,h1,h2) --- g is set to the intersection of h1 and h2.
 The vertex set of g is intersection of the vertex sets of h1 and h2, and
 the edge set of g is the intersection of the edge sets of h1 and h2.
