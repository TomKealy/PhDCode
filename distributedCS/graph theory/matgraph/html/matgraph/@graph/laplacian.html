laplacian

PURPOSE

laplacian(g) --- get the Laplacian matrix of g

SYNOPSIS

function m = laplacian(g)

DESCRIPTION 

 laplacian(g) --- get the Laplacian matrix of g
 equal to D-A where A is the adjacency matrix and D is a diagonal matrix
 of the degrees of the vertices.

  • nv nv(g) --- number of vertices in g
